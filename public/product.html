SkyRocket

The best teacher evaluation software in the Milky Way!

Features

Putting tools in your hands wherever you are.

SkyRocket brings the convenience and power of cloud storage companies like Dropbox and Google to educators by offering accessibility on every device you own. You can use our iPad app in the classroom, communicate with a student on your iPhone and then edit your observation at home on your desktop. We even developed offline mode, so you never have to worry about whether or not a school has wifi. No matter where you are, SkyRocket is by your side!

Giving you the collaboration tools you need.

Our discussion tool allows everyone to stay on the same page, whether it is a student-teacher, a faculty advisor or administrative staff at your teacher education program. It's not that we have anything against Gmail, we just believe that the most efficient way to communicate is by staying organized and that's what SkyRocket brings to the table. And by staying in touch with your student-teachers and advisors, you can spend less time catching up and more time doing what you do best.

Your documents stored where they belong -- in the cloud!

Our document storage system is as powerful as it is user-friendly. With the click of a button you'll be submitting, editing or reviewing a document without the hassle of downloading PDFs and emailing them to your administrative staff. And because every school's teacher education program is different, we built a "Create a Template" tool, so you can customize SkyRocket however you like. Our document storage tool, and for that matter SkyRocket as a whole, are full of features custom-made for teacher education. Give us a call or send us a chat message if you want to learn more!

Our Process

Every team needs a solid plan.

We spent three months doing nothing but talking to educators to find out what they needed most. Then we planned, made a plan, and did a bit more planning!

And products needs to simply work.

We have a pretty simple belief that every product should work. We believe in speed, simplicity and general ease of use. For the Apollosoft team, function and form are one in the same but if we had to choose we'd vote function over form.

And work simply :)

SkyRocket's user interface was designed with all users in mind. We brought the beauty of Twitter and the simplicity of Dropbox to the table and developed the most beautiful application on this side of the Milky Way!

Frequently asked questions

Can I pilot SkyRocket?

Absolutely! After your free trial, you can pilot SkyRocket for a semester to see how great it is. The cost of doing so will depend on how many students you have. Contact us for more details!

How do I sign up?

If you want to take a peak at the software feel free to sign up for a free trial. You can do this by visiting the homepage and clicking Get Skyrocket in the top right. This will take you to a page which will ask you to enter your email and desired password. Don't worry, we don't spam :)

What do you mean by users?

You may have noticed on our pricing that the cost is dependent on users. This is calculated by taking into account how many people will be using the software. For K-12 this is 250 and for Self-Evaluation it is 4.

Is my data secure?

You betcha! We know that data security is a top concern in the education field so we don't take the issue very lightly. All data is stored in secure data centers in Vancouver, BC. These servers are compliant with FIPPA and HIPPA.

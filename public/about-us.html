About Us

We believe in empowering teachers with tools that let them change lives.

Our belief is very simple. The affect of a small change on a vector early in its path results in huge changes in the long term. In education, the ability to affect thousands of vectors inspires our team to come to work everyday.

We are problem solvers. We are a team of developers, entrepreneurs and strategic thinkers making the world of software a better place; one that is full of a lot less pissed off, computer smashing users and a few more thankful, smiling customers. We work with, hire and hangout with people who say no to careers and yes to their passion.
